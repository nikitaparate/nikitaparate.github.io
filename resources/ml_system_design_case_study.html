ML System Design Case Studies

The field of machine learning (ML) has grown exponentially over the past decade, transforming industries and reshaping the way businesses operate. As organizations increasingly adopt ML technologies, the need for effective system design becomes paramount. Understanding how to architect robust, scalable, and efficient ML systems is crucial for leveraging the full potential of these technologies.

This article presents a comprehensive collection of 20 ML system design case studies, each providing unique insights into the challenges and solutions encountered in real-world applications. By examining these case studies, we aim to highlight the diverse approaches and best practices employed by leading companies and institutions in deploying ML systems.
